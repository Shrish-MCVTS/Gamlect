Our Purpose

Our project focuses on SDG #17: Life Below Water. Only one in a million gametes turn in a fully-fledged coral reef, our design will collect these gametes assisting the coral reef restoration.

Why Gamlect?

Innovative

Our design uses inspiration from the Troping Funnel Mushroom to help the restoration.

Enviroment Friendly

Our design is made out of a enviroment friendly material and is sustainable.

Efficient

Our design can collect signifcant amounts of gametes per spawning event.
